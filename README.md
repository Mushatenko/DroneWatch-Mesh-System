The system does not rely on the internet, GPS, cellular networks, servers, or cloud services.

##  Purpose

DroneWatch is designed for:

- detecting UAVs, drones, and airborne or surface anomalous objects;

##  Conceptual Model

DroneWatch uses a distributed approach in which each node:

- observes the local environment;  
- detects thermal, acoustic, vibrational, and other changes;  
- identifies events related to fires, floods, UAVs, and ground activity;  
- evaluates the significance of the event;  
- transmits information to neighboring nodes;  
- maintains overall network consistency.  

The interaction model ensures that the network remains stable  
even under partial loss, interference, or lack of direct visibility.
